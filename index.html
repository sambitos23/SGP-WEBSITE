ABOUT US

Where practical skill meets steady guidance

Siliguri Government Polytechnic focuses on enhancing students' knowledge, innovation, and technical skills. The institute offers seven diploma courses—five in Engineering & Technology, one in Architecture & Planning, and one in Pharmacy. Most courses are 3-year semester-based programs, while Pharmacy is a 2-year annual program. The intake is 60 students per course, except for Architecture Assistantship and Electrical Engineering, which have 30 seats each.
